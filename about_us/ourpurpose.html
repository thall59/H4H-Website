Our Purpose

At Hammocks for the Homeless we understand that poverty is an extremely complex subject. We also believe that systemic change can only happen when communities come together and care for one another. Our mission is simple: connect our communities with our homeless brothers and sisters. When we tap into individual stories and souls, we can begin to understand the root causes of poverty and start making the small changes that lead to systematic change.
