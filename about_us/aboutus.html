About Us

Following the One for One Model and focusing on the Metro Atlanta area, for every one hammock you purchase, we will give a hammock to a homeless person in need of a cozy place to sleep. Our partnerships with existing organizations enable us to get the hammocks into the hands of the ones who need them most. The revenue we receive from hammock sales will first go towards our program expenses, and the excess will go to our fundraisers and events that seek to raise support and awareness not only for Hammocks for the Homeless, but for our partners and the fight against homelessness as well.

 

There are a great deal of organizations that seek to do the same thing as we, and that is to end homelessness. However, there are still over 5,000 unsheltered homeless people on a given night in Georgia. Obviously there is room, and a need for more help on this issue. That is exactly what Hammocks for the Homeless intends to do; to help those who don’t have a cozy place to sleep, help those organizations that are doing everything they can to help those in need, and help the community become more involved with this fight against homelessness.

 

 

 

Our Team

Founder: Savannah Locke

“I am fueled by a love for people, philosophy, The Beatles, and Love itself. I believe that each individual is a universe within themselves, worthy of exploration and care. It is my life mission to relieve suffering for people as much as I can. If one person’s life is better because of my work, then it will all be worth it.”

 

 

Director: Joshua Mbugua

"Everybody has a story, and a unique story at that. Just because a person has reached a rough patch in this journey we call life does not mean that the story needs to screech to a halt. Sometimes people need assistance to turn the page and move on to the next chapter. It is one of my passions to analyze the economic conditions of the world that have resulted in the state of homelessness we have today and one day fix these problems on a global scale."
